Email Setup Guide

To set up your email:
1. Open your email client (Outlook, Gmail, etc.)
2. Go to Settings or Preferences
3. Select 'Add Account'
4. Choose 'IMAP' or 'POP3'
5. Enter server settings:
   - IMAP: imap.company.com
   - SMTP: smtp.company.com
   - Port: 993 (IMAP) or 587 (SMTP)
6. Enter your username and password
7. Test the connection

Contact IT support if you need specific server settings.
